Deep Dive Analysis

Why specialized solutions outperform general infrastructure.

Ad Precision

General proxies often miss dynamically loaded ads. Pangolin's Data Pilot engine is specifically trained to detect and parse Sponsored Brand/Video units, preserving absolute placement ranking for SOV analysis.

Smart Retries

Instead of manual retry logic (which costs engineering time), recent benchmarks show Pangolin's internal AI routing solves 99.2% of CAPTCHAs automatically before returning a response.

Cost Model

Consumption-based pricing means you pay for successful data, not bandwidth. For high-bandwidth targets like Amazon product pages (many images), this results in ~20% of the TCO compared to bandwidth-based proxies.
